Breaking Mental Health Stigma in India

Mental health remains a significant taboo in Indian society, creating barriers for young adults and students seeking support. With intense academic and social pressures, youth often lack accessible, non-judgmental outlets for their mental health concerns.

Sahara bridges this gap by providing an AI-powered, culturally sensitive platform that offers immediate support without the fear of stigma or judgment.
